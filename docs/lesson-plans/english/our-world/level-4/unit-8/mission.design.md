# Mission Design: Enjoy a Hobby (Unit 8)

## The Age-13 Spine
The emotional heart of this deck is the mirror between Leo and Jørn Hurum. At age 13, Leo is at the exact age Hurum was when he discovered a museum that transformed his "lonely" hobby into a future career. We move from the "alone" feeling of a unique interest to the "connected" world of sharing ideas, proving that Leo's interests (Soccer, Marvel, etc.) are valid seeds for his future.

## Vocabulary Plan
New words are taught lightly via visual mini-games. Academic language is "embodied" to anchor the lesson's objective.

| Word | Emoji | One-line definition |

| **paleontologist** | 👨‍🔬 | A scientist who studies fossils. |

## Solo Adaptation (Leo ↔ Dad)
No "Pair/Share" instruction survives.
1. **Think**: Leo reflects on prompts with frames.
2. **Pair**: Dad acts as the scribe/listener. Dad takes notes while Leo explains his hobby plan.
3. **Share**: Leo "presents" using his sequence frames. Dad uses his notes to ask one "Curious Dad" follow-up question.
