Used to for past habits

Use used to + verb to describe something that was true or happened regularly in the past but isn't true or doesn't happen anymore.

Where did you use to play?I used to play in the park.
Did you use to play?Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.
Used to is different from be used to.
I used to do homework at night = I did this regularly in the past, but not anymore.
I am used to doing homework at night = I do this regularly now. It is not a problem for me.
