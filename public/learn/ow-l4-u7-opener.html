BE THE EXPERT — About the Photo
Background knowledge for Dad to share with Leo
Be the Expert
About the Photo
🥽🌍
Invention: Space Projection Helmet
Event: NatGeo "One Strange Rock" premiere
Location: Washington, D.C., USA
Date: March 14, 2018
What Happened
At the first public showing of National Geographic's "One Strange Rock" TV series, journalists were given Space Projection Helmets — a special VR invention — so they could experience what it feels like to be an astronaut in space. It was one of the first times this technology was shown to the public.
The Invention
Space Projection Helmets use VR (virtual reality) technology. The problem they solve: most people can never go to space. The solution: put on a helmet and feel like you're there! This is what makes it a great invention — it solves a problem creatively.
Discussion Prompts
💡 "What problem does this invention solve?"
💡 "Can you think of another invention that uses VR or technology to solve a problem?"
💡 "Would you like to wear one?"

BE THE EXPERT — Teaching Tip
Teaching vocabulary in context
Be the Expert
Teaching Tip — Vocabulary in Context
Teach new vocabulary words in the context of a problem. For example, before defining "solution," ask: "Edison had a problem — people couldn't see in the dark. What do you think he needed?" Let Leo guess before giving the word. This builds understanding, not just memorisation.
❓
Start with the problem
Ask "What's the problem?" before introducing the word "solution" — Leo discovers the need for the word
💬
Use it in sentences
Model sentences with the new word first, then ask Leo to use it — "What's YOUR solution?"
🎯
Connect to real life
Ask: "What problems do YOU have at home that an invention could solve?" — personalise the vocabulary
