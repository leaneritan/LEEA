📐 The Rule
Two patterns — same meaning. 6 mini-quiz checks.
REMEMBER
Pattern A: verb + D.O. + to/for + I.O.
Pattern B: verb + I.O. + D.O.
Same meaning — different order!
D.O. answers WHAT? · I.O. answers TO/FOR WHOM?
Verbs with to: give, show, send, tell, write
Verbs with for: buy, make
