💎 WHAT ARE GEODES?
💎
Geodes are beautiful round rocks with crystals inside. They look ordinary on the outside!
🏞️
You can find geodes near rivers. People collect them as a hobby.
📅
Geodes take about 240 million years to form. The crystals grow very slowly inside.
🗣️
The student in the photo says: "This is the coolest rock I have! I bought it on vacation."
