HIDE and SEEK
Paragraph 1 of 4
Geocaching is a fun outdoor hobby. It's like a game of hide and seek, with the hiders leaving clues for the seekers to find. Here's how it works: The hiders hide a box, or cache. Inside the box, they put some "treasure" — small objects for you to find — and a notebook. If you want to be a seeker, or geocacher, you use an app that tells you where there are caches near you.
