Unit 8·Grammar 2 — Today's Goal📖 LP p.274
🎯 Today's Learning Goals
🎯 OBJECTIVE
Identify and use direct and indirect objects in a sentence.
📐 TWO PATTERNS — SAME MEANING
PATTERN A Bellingham gave the trophy to Xabi Alonso.
PATTERN B Bellingham gave Xabi Alonso the trophy.
🔑 KEY QUESTIONS
WHAT? = the direct object 🎁
TO/FOR WHOM? = the indirect object 👤
🎓 ACADEMIC WORDS
🎁 direct object — the THING (or person) receiving the action
👤 indirect object — the person TO/FOR whom the action happens
⚠️ TODAY'S VERBS — 7 only
With TO: give, show, send, tell, write
With FOR: buy, make
🔄 RECYCLED — V2 words land as D.O.s
🐞📚🦖🪨🧸 — bug · comic book · dinosaur · fossil · stuffed animal. All can be the THING someone gives, shows, sends or buys.

📌 Direct and indirect objects · TR 8.6
SHOW · Show the trophy to the manager. = Show the manager the trophy.
GIVE · Bellingham gave the armband to Vinicius. = Bellingham gave Vinicius the armband.
BUY · The club bought new boots for the squad. = The club bought the squad new boots.
💬 Dad: "Listen to TR 8.6. Repeat each pair. Notice — both sentences mean the same thing. The order changed, that's all. buy uses for; give/show use to."

Unit 8·The TWO PATTERNS — visualized📖 Grammar in Depth
📐 Two patterns — same meaning
PATTERN A
subject + verb + D.O. + to/for + I.O.
Bellingham gave the trophy to Xabi Alonso.
The president bought new boots for the squad.
Vinicius showed his medal to the fans.
⇄
PATTERN B
subject + verb + I.O. + D.O.
⚠️ no to/for!
Bellingham gave Xabi Alonso the trophy.
The president bought the squad new boots.
Vinicius showed the fans his medal.
✅ Same meaning, different order. Pattern A puts the THING first + uses to/for. Pattern B puts the PERSON first + drops to/for.

Unit 8·Two Questions, Two Answers📖 Grammar in Depth
🚩 Two question flags — WHAT? · TO/FOR WHOM?
Courtois sent his gloves to a young keeper.
🚩 WHAT? = direct object 🎁
What did Courtois send? → his gloves
A thing! The action lands on it.
🚩 TO WHOM? = indirect object 👤
To whom did Courtois send the gloves? → a young keeper
A person! The receiver.
📐 Try this on every double-object sentence: ask WHAT? and TO/FOR WHOM? — those are your two objects!

💬 Dad: "Pick the correct Pattern B rewrite. Correct = Trophy moment #7 unlocks!"

Yamal sent to a young fan a Barcelona jersey.
Yamal sent a young fan a Barcelona jersey.

Yamal sent for a young fan a Barcelona jersey.
📐 Pattern B = verb + I.O. + D.O. — no to, no for.

Unit 8·What We Learned Today📖 Summary
🎓 Three rules to remember
📐
RULE 1 — Two patterns
A verb + D.O. + to/for + I.O.
B verb + I.O. + D.O.
Same meaning!
🚩
RULE 2 — Two questions
WHAT? = D.O. 🎁
TO/FOR WHOM? = I.O. 👤
Ask both to find both!
⚠️
RULE 3 — Verb picks the prep
to: give, show, send, tell, write
for: buy, make
7 verbs total — that's the lesson!
