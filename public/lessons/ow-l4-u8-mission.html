Content Vocabulary 📖

fossil 🦴
The remains of a plant or animal from long ago.
ammonite 🐚
An extinct sea animal with a ring-shaped shell.
paleontologist 👨‍🔬
A scientist who studies fossils.
Extinct, Skeleton, Sea Reptile.

Sea Giant Vocabulary 🦖

extinct 🦖
No longer living; died out long ago.
skeleton 💀
The frame of bones inside a body.
sea reptile 🐉
A large animal that lived in the ocean.
Pliosaur, Prey.

The Hunt 🦈

pliosaur 🦈
A giant, hunter sea reptile (like "Predator X").
prey 🥩
An animal that is hunted and eaten by another.
Ammonite fossils. Squid-like, ring shells, extinct ~65 mya.

About the Photo 🐚

🐚🐚🐚
These are ammonite fossils.
They were squid-like creatures that lived inside ring-shaped shells.
They lived in groups millions of years ago!

Fossil Facts ⏳

🦖
Ammonites went extinct at the same time as the dinosaurs.
📅
Scientists use them to date other fossils. They tell us how old rocks are!
Jørn Hurum. Norwegian Paleontologist. Arctic dig.
