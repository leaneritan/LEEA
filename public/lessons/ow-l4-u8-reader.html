🎓 The Paleontologist

Jørn became a paleontologist — a scientist who studies fossils and ancient life. He became a professor at the Natural History Museum in Oslo.

🏛️ His work today

For decades, he has enjoyed his work. He gives lectures, leads tours, and has escorted thousands of visitors through the museum exhibits, showing them fossils that are millions of years old.

🐒 The big discovery!

His biggest discovery was a primate fossil called "Ida" that is 47 million years old! He is a man who turned a childhood hobby into a career related to what he has always loved.

  • What was his biggest discovery?

Q1: What was Jørn Hurum's hobby as a child? ▼
Jørn Hurum's hobby was collecting fossils. He found them in the woods near his home in Oslo, Norway.
Q2: What book changed his life? Why? ▼
He found a book about fossils. After reading it alone in his room, he started looking for fossils everywhere — under rocks, near rivers, and in the forest. It sparked his lifelong passion.
Q3: What is a paleontologist? ▼
A paleontologist is a scientist who studies fossils and ancient life. Jørn became a paleontologist and professor at the Natural History Museum in Oslo.
Q4: What was Jørn's biggest discovery? ▼
His biggest discovery was a primate fossil called "Ida" that is 47 million years old!
Q5: How did Jørn turn his hobby into a career? ▼
He studied at university to learn the proper way to find and study fossils. He became a professor who gives lectures, leads tours, and shows fossil exhibits to thousands of visitors.
