Unit 8·BE THE EXPERT — Vocabulary Strategy
⭐ BE THE EXPERT — Vocabulary Strategy: Using Context
Using context means reading the words, phrases, and sentences around an unfamiliar word. Surrounding text often contains clues to meaning, such as examples or definitions.
📌 Try it with "alone":
"Many video games are for one person. You play alone."

The sentence before says: "for one person" → this helps you understand that alone means by yourself!
🧑 Leo's Turn: Find the word "creative" on page 132. Read the sentences around it. What clues help you understand what creative means?
Unit 8·BE THE EXPERT — Teaching Tip
💡 BE THE EXPERT — Teaching Tip: Brainstorming
Brainstorming with others is a good way to get original and creative ideas. When you brainstorm together, listen to each other's ideas. Write ideas first, then decide which are best.
⏱️ Set a time limit for brainstorming!
1. Leo writes ALL ideas — no judgment yet (2 minutes)
2. Together, look at the list — which ideas are best?
3. Choose the best idea and develop it!
💬 Dad asks: "What new hobby would you like to try? Brainstorm 5 ideas in 2 minutes!"
