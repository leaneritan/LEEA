🎮 Real Dinosaur? (Long Ago) or Toy?

🦖 All dinosaurs lived MILLIONS of years ago. None today!
🪨
Word 4 of 5 — a fossil
LP: 'Fossils can be marks or bones from animals in the past.'
4/5
🪨
a fossil
Noun · Target
DEFINITION
the hard remains or mark of a plant or animal from long ago
化石（かせき）
Leo — fossils are how scientists know about dinosaurs! A trilobite is a famous old fossil (Unit 8 academic word!).

🎮 Fossil or Not?

Fossils = ancient marks or bones in stone. Tap the fossils.
🪨 Fossils show life from long ago. Stone-preserved remains!
🧸
Word 5 of 5 — a stuffed animal
LP: 'A teddy bear is a kind of stuffed animal.'
5/5
🧸
a stuffed animal
Noun phrase · Target
DEFINITION
a soft toy animal filled with material
ぬいぐるみ
Leo — Sid the sloth from Ice Age, the Marvel heroes' plushies… stuffed animal toys of every favorite character exist!

🎮 Stuffed Animal or Not?

SOFT, FILLED toy = stuffed animal. Click them.
🧸 Stuffed animals are soft + filled + animal-shaped!
