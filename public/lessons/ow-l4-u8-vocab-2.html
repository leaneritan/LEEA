Unit 8·Vocabulary 2 — Strategy📖 LP p.272 · TR 8.5
💡 Vocabulary Strategy: Use Context Clues
When you see a NEW WORD, look at the words and pictures around it. The context tells you what the word means!
✅ "Maria has a small box. There is a bug on the leaf inside." → small + leaf = an insect 🐞
✅ "My brother reads a comic book. He laughs at Spider-Man!" → reads + Spider-Man = book with pictures 📚
✅ "The museum has a dinosaur. It is very, very big!" → museum + big = animal from long ago 🦖
✅ "I found a fossil in the rock. It looks like a fish from long ago." → rock + long ago = old animal mark 🪨
✅ "My little sister hugs her stuffed animal at night." → hugs + little sister = soft toy 🧸
Look for:
🖼️ Pictures
🔤 Other words nearby
💬 Who is talking
📦 Where it happens

Unit 8·Academic Word — Accuracy 🎯🎓 Academic
🎯 Academic Word: ACCURACY
DEFINITION
Accuracy = how correct or exact something is.
正確さ（せいかくさ）
"Check your spelling for accuracy."
💬 Dad: "Accuracy is not about speed. It is about getting it RIGHT."
💬 Leo: "Like Salah's shot accuracy — 8.7 shots on target per match this season ⚽🎯"
💡 WHEN DO WE USE "ACCURACY"?
  • 📝 Check your spelling for accuracy
  • 📊 Check your answers for accuracy
  • 🎯 Check your aim for accuracy (sports!)
  • 📚 Check your reading for accuracy
🎯 Quick check:
Which action helps accuracy?
⚽ Pep Guardiola says accuracy > speed. "A pass that's 1 metre off is wasted!"
