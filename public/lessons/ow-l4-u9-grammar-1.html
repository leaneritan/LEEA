Unit 9·Grammar in Depth — Cause or Effect?📖 LP p.299 · Be the Expert
🔬 Sort the sentence halves!
CAUSE = it happens first and makes the other happen · EFFECT = the result
"One skater pulls"
"the other one spins"
"you push the swing"
"it goes faster"
First half = CAUSE ➡️ Second half = EFFECT

Unit 9·The Comma Law 🔍📖 LP p.299 · Be the Expert
🔍 Comma Inspector: tap the box where the comma goes!
⚖️ LAB LAW: a comma , always separates the two halves.
The more he pushes down the higher he goes.
The more you play soccer the better you get.
💡 Hint: the comma stands exactly where half 1 ends and half 2 begins.
Unit 9·Danger Zone — the good/better trap📖 LP p.300
⚠️ One of these is a TRAP. Choose carefully…
"The more you play soccer, the ______ you get."
🚨
good → better. Always.
"gooder" is NOT a word — it's a trapdoor! Same family: bad → worse.

Always start with The more (or The higher…)

high → higher · fast → faster · good → better 🚨
