⭐ Be the Expert — Vocabulary Strategy
Antonyms. Antonyms are words with opposite meanings, such as hot and cold. Learning words in antonym pairs can help students categorize and understand new vocabulary words.

To teach antonym pairs that include verbs, act out the opposite verbs for students. For other antonym pairs, display two sentences with opposite meanings.

Students can write the sentences in a vocabulary log and refer to them later. In this unit, antonym pairs include backward/forward, push/pull, and balance/fall down.
💡 Be the Expert — Teaching Tip
If a student is having difficulty with an assignment, avoid jumping in too quickly to help. It's natural for a student to struggle with learning something new — students will feel more of a sense of accomplishment when they do something difficult on their own.

Give students enough time to work through the activity on their own, even if they find it difficult to do so. If they are still struggling, then offer help.
