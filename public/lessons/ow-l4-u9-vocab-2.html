Word 1&2 / 5·toward & away from — Meaning📇 cards
➡️
toward
moving in the direction of something — getting closer to it
🇯🇵 〜のほうへ  ·  "Mbappé runs toward the goal."
⬅️ OPPOSITES ➡️
⬅️
away from
moving in the other direction — getting farther from something
🇯🇵 〜からはなれて  ·  "The goalkeeper kicks the ball away from the goal."

Word 3 / 5·direction — Meaning📇 card
🧭
direction
the way that someone or something moves or points (up, down, left, right, forward…)
🇯🇵 方向（ほうこう）  ·  "Turn your wheel in the direction you want to go."
🚴 On the bike photo: to go left, you steer your wheel in that direction — and you lean that way too! (next word 👀)

Word 4 / 5·lean — Meaning📇 card
🙆
lean
to move your body a little to one side
🇯🇵 (からだを)かたむける  ·  "The biker leans to help her turn left."
🏍️ Real life: bikers, skateboarders, and even soccer players lean when they change direction fast!

Word 5 / 5·gravity — Meaning📇 card
🍎⬇️
gravity
the force that pulls everything down toward the ground
🇯🇵 重力（じゅうりょく）  ·  "When you throw a ball, gravity pulls it toward the Earth."
🚀 Space fact: in space there is almost NO gravity — so astronauts and their soccer balls FLOAT! What would a penalty kick be like up there? 🤔
