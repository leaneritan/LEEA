Training·Why We Train Today🎯 the mission
🎯 The problem: English vowels play tricks!
🇯🇵 JAPANESE VOWELS — honest players
あ い う え お
5 sounds. They NEVER change. あ is always あ!
🇬🇧 ENGLISH VOWELS — tricky players
a e i o u
5 letters… but each letter can make DIFFERENT sounds! 😱
👀 SEE THE TRICK
cat 🐱 → "ah-eh" short sound
cake 🎂 → "ay" long sound
Same letter a — two different sounds!

Drill 2 / 6·Short e — the EGG sound 🥚🔶 Cone 2

Drill 3 / 6·Short i — the PIG sound 🐷🔶 Cone 3
i
🐷
/ɪ/ — like pig! Quick and lazy "ih"
👄 Mouth almost closed, relaxed · NOT Japanese い — shorter and lazier! (sit ≠ seat!)
🎯 DRILL: Tap every word with the PIG sound! (3 to find)
🔶 Cone 3 conquered! sit, win, kick — ih ih ih! 🐷⚽
Drill 4 / 6·Short o — the OCTOPUS sound 🐙🔶 Cone 4
o
🐙
/ɒ/ — like hot! Round open "o(ah)"
👄 Round lips, jaw DROPS · deeper and shorter than Japanese お
🎯 DRILL: Tap every word with the OCTOPUS sound! (3 to find)
🔶 Cone 4 conquered! hot, top, stop! 🐙
Drill 5 / 6·Short u — the UMBRELLA sound ☂️🔶 Cone 5
u
☂️
/ʌ/ — like cup! A lazy little "uh"
👄 Mouth barely opens · tongue relaxed at the BACK · NOT Japanese う — no round lips!
🎯 DRILL: Tap every word with the UMBRELLA sound! (3 to find)
🔶 Cone 5 conquered! run, sun, cut — uh uh uh! ☂️
