About the Photo 🐚

🐚🐚🐚
These are ammonite fossils.
They were squid-like creatures that lived inside ring-shaped shells.
They lived in groups millions of years ago!
Key facts: Extinct ~65 mya. Used to date other fossils. "If you find an ammonite, you know how old the rock is!"

Fossil Facts ⏳

🦖
Ammonites went extinct at the same time as the dinosaurs (~65 million years ago).
📅
Scientists use them to date other fossils. They tell us how old the rocks around them are!
