Describing people with who

OW4-U8-G1GRAMMAR POINTLIVE LESSON
Use who to give more information about a person.
person who verb phrase
The boy who collects fossils is my friend.
A photographer is a person who takes photos.
She is the girl who likes soccer.
who を使って人を説明する

人について、さらに説明を加えるときに who を使います。

形: 人 + who + 動詞句
